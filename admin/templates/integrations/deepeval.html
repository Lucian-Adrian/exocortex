RAG Metrics Explained

âœ… Faithfulness

Measures whether the answer is grounded in the retrieved context. High faithfulness = No hallucination. The model only says things it found in the documents.

Score: 0.0 (hallucinating) to 1.0 (fully grounded)

ğŸ¯ Answer Relevancy

Measures how relevant the answer is to the original question. Does the response actually address what was asked?

Score: 0.0 (off-topic) to 1.0 (perfectly relevant)

ğŸ“š Contextual Recall

Measures whether the retrieval found all relevant documents. Did we retrieve everything we needed to answer the question?

Score: 0.0 (missing docs) to 1.0 (found all relevant)

ğŸ”¬ Contextual Precision

Measures whether retrieved documents are actually relevant. Did we retrieve only what we needed, without noise?

Score: 0.0 (lots of noise) to 1.0 (all relevant)
